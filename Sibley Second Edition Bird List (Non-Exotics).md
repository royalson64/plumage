# Sibley Second Edition Bird List (Non-Exotics)

> Regularly occurring **native** birds of North America (north of Mexico), organized
> taxonomically by family. Derived from the *North American Core Native Birds Checklist*,
> which captures the ~780 regularly occurring native species via representative species
> per family. Used as the reference for curating the Plumage bird catalog.

*This document compiles the major families and key species making up the ~780 regularly occurring native birds of North America north of Mexico, structured taxonomically following standard North American ornithological consensus.*
